DeckBoss

Voice-first fishing log. No signal needed. Free.

Tap to record. Timestamp, GPS, and transcript happen automatically. Works offline. No account. No DeckBoss server ever holds your logs.

Open DeckBoss — Free, No Signup View the code on GitHub

How it works

  1. Tap the record button.

    One big button, built for gloves and wet hands. No gestures to fumble.

  2. Talk.

    Say what you caught, where, and how. The transcript is generated automatically.

  3. Done.

    Timestamp and GPS save automatically. Even with zero bars, your capture is stored locally; the transcript catches up when connectivity returns.

Works offline, by design

Zero signal is the normal operating environment on a working boat, not an edge case. Entries queue locally and sync when you get back in range.

Your storage, your rules

Sync to your own Google Drive, Cloudflare R2, Oracle Object Storage, or export a plain .zip. No DeckBoss server sits in the middle.

Nothing is deleted

Entries are amended or retracted through additive corrections. The original capture stays recoverable.

What it looks like on deck

Your data, not ours

  • No account required.
  • No DeckBoss server ever holds your logs.
  • Cloud sync uses credentials you control in Settings.
  • Open source under the MIT license.

What this is not

Not a catch-reporting tool. DeckBoss is a personal log for your own records. It is not a substitute for official or regulatory catch reporting, and it has not been reviewed or certified as evidence-grade for legal or insurance purposes.

No hardware, payment, or support SLA. This is a working beta, not a supported product. Use your own phone. If something breaks, we fix it when we can.

Don't bet your real records on it yet. Week one of the beta asks you to keep your current logging method running in parallel, so a DeckBoss failure costs you nothing.

Field beta

We're recruiting 3–5 working boats for a 6–8 week on-the-water test.

Participation means a one-time setup session, logging at least once daily in real conditions, and a short daily check-in by call or text. No hardware, no payment, no support SLA.
